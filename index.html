About Us

Al-Nisa Maternity & General Hospital

Al-Nisa Maternity & General Hospital is a trusted healthcare institution located in Noor Bagh, Gulbarga (Kalaburagi).


We provide comprehensive maternity care, gynecology, newborn care and general healthcare services with a patient-first approach.


Our mission is to deliver quality healthcare while ensuring comfort, safety and personalized treatment for every patient.

Our Services

Maternity Care

Pregnancy consultation, antenatal care, normal delivery, C-section and postnatal support.

Gynecology

Women's health checkups, PCOS management, fertility counseling and family planning.

General Healthcare

General physician consultations, diagnostics and preventive healthcare.

Newborn Care

Infant care, newborn assessments and growth monitoring.
